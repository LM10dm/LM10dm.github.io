Plants of Power

Plants of Power is a practical guide to organic plant cultivation, combining science-backed gardening techniques with intuitive plant identification. It teaches readers how to grow medicinal, edible, and rare plants using sustainable methods like composting and rainwater harvesting. The book also offers a unique approach to recognizing plants in nature without memorization, focusing on natural observation and environmental patterns. Perfect for gardeners of all levels, Plants of Power empowers readers to create thriving, organic gardens while deepening their connection with the natural world.
